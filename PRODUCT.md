# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Product marketers, founders, growth teams, consultants, and AI product builders who need to turn a product idea or mature product into structured user insight, emotional drivers, and actionable product opportunities. They are usually working under time pressure and need a credible report they can use in strategy meetings, product reviews, or client-facing work.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, premium. The product should feel like a quiet professional instrument rather than a playful generator: refined, precise, focused, and worth paying for.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS visuals, gradient-heavy dashboards, colorful badge overload, decorative glassmorphism, emoji-driven interfaces, marketing-page hero theatrics, and cramped form builders. The interface should not feel like a template marketplace, a chat toy, or a low-cost prompt wrapper.

## Design Principles

- Quiet confidence: let structure, spacing, and typography carry the sense of value.
- Task before spectacle: every control must support the analysis workflow directly.
- Expert output deserves expert framing: reports should look boardroom-ready, not like raw generated text.
- Restraint is the brand: use color and emphasis only when they clarify hierarchy or state.
- Mobile is not secondary: the same professional rhythm must survive narrow screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Maintain visible focus states, keyboard-accessible controls, reduced-motion support, and layouts that do not rely on color alone for meaning.
